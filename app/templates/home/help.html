Help


  1. Register
    • Direction: Enter your credentials and press register
    • Warning: If there is an error, the program will tell you after you press the button
    • Warning: If the page is not registering, check if your google play store is up to date 11.6.10 or higher
  2. Login
    • Direction: Enter your credentials and press login
    • Direction: If you forgot your password, press forgot password
    • Direction: If you are a new user, press new user
    • Warning: If there is an error, the program will tell you after you press the button
    • Warning: If the page is not Logging in, check if your google play store is up to date 11.6.10 or higher
  3. Welcome Page
    • Direction: If you want to search for films, press find my films
    • Direction: If you want to view or edit details of your account, press find my account
    • Direction: If you want to logout, press find my logout
  4. Search Page
    • Direction: Enter your ideal attributes you want your film to have credentials and press Find My Film
  5. Results Page
    • Info: After pressing Find My Film on the Search page, you will be lead to this page.
    • Direction: Select the film you will like to learn more about, and you will be directed to the Film detail page
  6. Film Details Page
    • Info: After selecting a film on the Results page, you will be lead to this page to view details of your film
  7. My Account Page
    • Direction: If you want to edit a credential, under the ideal credentials press edit an enter your new credentials.
    • Direction: If you want to save all changes, press update account
    • Warning: If there is an error, the program will tell you after you press the update account button
  8. Watchlist Page
    • Direction: If you want to delete a film from the list, press the delete button under the film
    • Direction: If you want to add a film on the list, press watch move button on film detail page
  9. Films I've Seen Page
    • Direction: If you want to delete a film from the list, press the delete button under the film
    • Direction: If you want to add a film on the list, press seen move button on film detail page
